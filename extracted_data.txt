Ortho slippers are designed with unique features and therapeutic detailing to offer the required comfort and protection for sensitive feet.
Benefits
People are more prone to suffer from ultra-sensitivity, pains, and swelling of the feet, especially if your daily work requires you to stand for long hours. The flexi puff of the orthopaedic slippers are comfortable for people with heel pain. These slippers are designed with the correct cushioning to provide optimum comfort to your tired feet. Underfoot comfort is built into the design of the slipper. Besides providing comfort, these slippers also come with proper grip to prevent you from slipping off and falling down.
